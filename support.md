## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need a Reddit account?**  
No. IdeaMine works without any external accounts.

**Does IdeaMine post content on my behalf?**  
No. IdeaMine is strictly read-only.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**  
Subscriptions are managed through iOS:
Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions
